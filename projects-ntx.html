Nanotank Water Exchange - NTX

Problem Space

A nano-tank, usually < 5 gallons, tend to be more difficult to maintain due to a reduced water volume. This limits its buffering capacity against waste products, and can cause a tank to crash out from chemical imbalances.


To prevent this, frequent water changes are required to exchange the waste-contaminated water with pure water. However, these changes also need to be minimally disruptive to the fish. This demands

  • < 25% total volume
  • Gentle exchanges

  • Time-consuming
  • Extra equipment
  • Stress to pet
